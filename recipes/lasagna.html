Lasagna



This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it! When John Chandler submitted this lasagna recipe to Allrecipes more than 20 years ago, he had no idea how successful it would become. Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
